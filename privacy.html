Privacy Policy

This page explains what data is collected on atrak.dev and how it’s used.

Information We Collect

  • Suggestion Box: the message you submit (no name required).
  • Join / Contact: name, email, selected interest area, and your message.
  • Security reports: vulnerability details, affected project, severity, and optional contact email.
  • Technical metadata: our form provider may log standard request metadata (like IP address and user agent) for abuse prevention and delivery.

How We Use Information

  • Review suggestions, bug reports, and feedback.
  • Investigate security reports and fix vulnerabilities.
  • Reply to join/contact messages using the email you provide.
  • Improve the site and our projects.

Where Submissions Go

Form submissions are sent through a third-party form provider (configured in config.js) and delivered to our team inbox. We don’t publish your submissions.

We do not sell your information.

Your Choices

  • Do not include sensitive personal data in forms.
  • If you want a message deleted, contact us via the form on join.html.
  • External links (like GitHub) have their own policies.
