AI Predator Simulation

A complex multi-agent survival simulation where entities learn to hunt, survive, and adapt in a dynamic ecological environment.

Overview

Simulates predator-prey dynamics using autonomous agents that react to environmental cues and internal states (hunger, health, proximity).

Project Manager: Charlie Han

Role: Lead Developer

Features

  • Emergent group hunting behaviors
  • Resource management & ecological balance
  • Dynamic pathfinding and evasion logic

Status

V1 completed in November 2025. Code is open-source and available for research and expansion into more complex AI architectures.
