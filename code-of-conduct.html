Community

Code of Conduct

Atrak is built by students. We keep our spaces respectful, safe, and focused on learning and shipping.

Our Pledge

We’re committed to providing a welcoming environment for everyone who participates in Atrak — whether you’re contributing code, sharing feedback, or collaborating on a build.

Expected Behavior

  • Be respectful and constructive in feedback.
  • Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
  • Keep discussions focused on the work.
  • Respect privacy — don’t share private info.
  • Help others learn: explain, mentor, and review kindly.

Unacceptable Behavior

  • Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, or threats.
  • Bullying, repeated personal attacks, or intimidation.
  • Posting private information without consent.
  • Spam, scams, or malicious links.
  • Disrupting collaboration or sabotaging work.

Reporting & Enforcement

  • If you need follow-up, use the form on Join and include contact info.
  • If you prefer anonymity, use the Suggestion Box.
  • For security issues, use Security.

We take reports seriously. The team may remove content, revoke access, or pause participation as needed to protect the community and the work.
